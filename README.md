# Gottlieb-Chime-Resonator
This repository contains STL files for fabricating a chime resonator for a EM Gottlieb pinball machine.  The design is a close approximation based on photos with a ruler.  The Gottlieb chime unit consist of three notes; 5F#(739.99Hz), 5D#(622.25Hz), and 5C# (554.37Hz).  The frequency for each of the resonating cavities is much lower than the notes produced by the chimes, the frequencies calculated are 510, 456, and 411 Hz. I'm not an expert on accuostics, but I suspect the deisgner of this resonator wanted to enhance the lower harmonics to give the chimes a much richer tone. If you want a resonator at the base ket frequencies, I have included the stl for those also, but they may not mount directly into a Gottlieb chime chassis, as they are much smaller in height and width.  
As mentioned the resonator consists of two parts, top and bottom. However there is an aperature riser that is glued to the top of the resonator, this is included too.
